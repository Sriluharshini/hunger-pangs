Who We Are

Hunger Pangs is a mission-driven initiative dedicated to bridging the gap between food excess and food scarcity. Across the globe, millions suffer from hunger and malnutrition, while tons of edible food go to waste — especially during events, gatherings, and restaurants. Our platform was built to create a smarter, more compassionate food ecosystem.

Our Vision

To create a world where no edible food is wasted and no human sleeps hungry. Hunger Pangs envisions a sustainable, community-driven model where every excess meal finds its way to someone in need.

Our Mission

Reduce Food Waste

Empower communities and event organizers to list surplus food easily and ensure it is utilized instead of discarded.

Fight Hunger

Connect donors with NGOs, shelters, volunteers, and individuals who can quickly claim and distribute excess food.

Build a Caring Community

Encourage collective responsibility where people actively participate in supporting those in need.

Why We Exist

Hunger Pangs was created to solve two massive global issues with one unified solution. By enabling real-time food redistribution, we help fight hunger while significantly cutting down food wastage. Our platform thrives on compassion, collaboration, and community-centered action.
